As a starting point, check out what I call my **identity stack**. These are the core things that make up who I am and guide everything I do. They flow in this order:

[[Beliefs]] → [[Values]] → [[Goals]] → [[Actions]] + [[Systems]] → [[Identities]]

Unfortunately, many of these terms are used interchangeable in society. You'll find my definition of these terms in each page.

This is a work in progress! I'm currently experimenting with Quartz, a subdomain for my garden, and using a separate Obsidian vault to make edits to it. The downsides of this are:
1. It's separate from my main website. Which is OK, I guess, but would be better to have everything together. I'll probably look into integrating the markdown into the main website later down the road.
2. There's an inherent version control issue between my private & public digital gardens, since they live on two different platforms (Notion & Obsidian). I won't move off Notion since it contains my entire life, and it's nice to be able to add additional private notes to pages published here. I guess that goes against the [ethos of the digital garden](https://maggieappleton.com/garden-history/), but I'm still figuring out my goals here.
