> Phrases I repeat to myself to give me strength when I need it.
## Seek Discomfort
* catch-all for anything uncomfortable
## Can’t Hurt Me
- when doing something that sucks
## Trust Your Systems
* a more personal version of "trust the process"
## Restrain Your Desires
* when I need the reminder that desire is suffering, and that I must be picky with my desires.

## Use the External Push
- motivation and energy are perishable, and this helps me capitalize when they are given externally
- there are fundamentally two modes of being, one where you’re intrinsically motivated, and are carrying yourself, and one where you’re extrinsically motivated and are being pushed by someone else
    - aim to be in the former, but recognize when you’re in the latter, and capitalize on it.

* when working on something tedious and wanting to quit.
